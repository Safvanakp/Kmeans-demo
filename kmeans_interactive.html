2. Elbow Method

Using the same points from the left canvas, this runs K-Means for K = 1 to 8 and plots WCSS (inertia) against K. The "elbow" — where the curve bends and flattens — suggests a good K.

Suggested K: – Needs points on the left canvas
How to read it: WCSS always decreases as K increases. Look for the point where adding another cluster stops giving a big drop — that bend is the "elbow," and it's usually the best trade-off between accuracy and simplicity.
